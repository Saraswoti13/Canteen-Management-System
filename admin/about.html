ABOUT US

Vojan Express revolutionizes the payment process in canteens by integrating RFID (Radio-Frequency Identification) technology with a sophisticated web-based platform. This innovative system allows students, staff, and visitors to make secure, cashless payments using RFID cards linked to individual accounts managed through a user-friendly web interface. Vojan Express ensures a fast and efficient checkout experience, reducing the risks associated with cash handling and minimizing transaction times. By leveraging advanced technology with comprehensive web integration, Vojan Express provides real-time updates on account balances and transaction histories, delivering a seamless, convenient, and secure experience for all users.
